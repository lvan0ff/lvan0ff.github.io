About Us

Alexander Antonov- I am the owner, brand strategist and commercial photographer at "Edge Photos" photography in Varna, Bulgaria.Found his true love in landscape and travel photography. Hee travels as often as possible making visual memories of moments in time. Knowing not everyone is blessed with seeing these sights in person, he decided to share his work with the world.

Evgeni Ivanov- He is Varna based photographer specialized in capturing the urban environments and the people who inhabit it. His passion for urban landscapes and architecture impel him to be a perfectionist and demanding with the compositions and the lines game. In turn, it makes his style somewhat "grunge'', strong and rough textures.
